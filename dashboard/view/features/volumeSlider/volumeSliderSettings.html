How It Works

  • Remember Volume Level: When enabled, your volume slider position is saved and applied to all videos
  • Remember Mute State: When enabled, muting/unmuting in one tab instantly syncs to all other Instagram tabs
  • If disabled, videos use Instagram's default volume (100%) and mute state
  • Changes apply instantly without page refresh
  • Perfect for maintaining consistent audio experience across all videos
