Story Blocking Mode

Choose how stories should be hidden on Instagram

Selective Hide

Hide stories only on specific screens (home page and stories screen)

Hide All Stories

Completely hide all stories from all users across Instagram

How It Works

  • Selective Hide: Stories will be hidden on the home page feed and when you visit the stories screen (/stories)
  • Hide All Stories: All story elements will be completely hidden across your entire Instagram experience
  • You can change this setting anytime from this page
